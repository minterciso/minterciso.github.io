Recreation of Asterisk infrastructure

About

With little to no knowledge of Telephony infrastructure, I was asked to recreate the Asterisk Infrastructure for the entire company, and had around 8 hours downtime only.

We were successful, and with a 6 hour downtime we:

  • Created the Asterisk server
  • Configured the E1 line
  • Updated and migrated the Jabber used at the time
  • Configured all extensions via UDP deploy

Challenges

Needless to say that this was a challenging task, but with the correct people to ask for information and the correct team to support the more repetitive tasks, this was a successful work, deemed of telling about at a bar!
